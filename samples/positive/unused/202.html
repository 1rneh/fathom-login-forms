What is Vodafone At Home?

Vodafone At Home is Vodafone's fixed line, broadband and TV service

Why consider Vodafone At Home?

  • We have a range of great value and worry free plans to suit everyone.
  • Unlimited broadband on all plans.
  • Vodafone TV, with all the channels you love and Netflix at the touch of a button. Plus a range of great features, such as wireless Multiroom, fast channel switching, restart TV and HD for free.
