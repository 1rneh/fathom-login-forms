  • Highest Quote for your Electronics

    We always strive to offer the highest dollar amount possible for your electronics. We understand that reselling a used iPhone or iPad can be tedious, especially with so many option to choose from. Many iPhone refurbishers, resellers and repair shops offer unrealistic amounts, then slash their quotes upon delivery. Therefore we recommend researching a company before doing business with them.     Your payment is issued within 24 hours of having your iPhone, smartphone, DVD player or gaming console delivered to us. Laptops and Macbooks may take up to 48 hours.

    Unfortunately, we cannot offer a full quote if your iPhone is still active, has unpaid bills, has been reported stolen or a has blacklisted IMEI/ESN.

  • What type of Electronics can I sell?

    We buy nearly any electronic device in any condition. From laptops, cell phones, and iPhones to GPS systems, mp3 players, video gaming consoles, we will make you an offer regardless of its condition. And as always, shipping is free.

  • Why is this my best option to sell my old electronics?

    While eBay and Craigslist are two good alternatives to our trade-in program, they do have their disadvantages. Using eBay can be risky, especially if you are not an experienced seller. Craigslist limits your buying audience to your town/city, and it can often take weeks for you to find the right buyer. With our service, you will receive a straight-forward quote, a simple shipping process, and that's it. No hassle, no risk, no worries.

  • What happens to my laptop or electronic device once I sell it?

    We buy used electronics so that we can extend their life by refurbishing them and reselling them through different marketplaces. With older electronics, mostly laptops, We scrap and separate its components by material type and send them to recycling facilities where they make sure that these materials are reused in the manufacturing of other products. All electronics are disposed of responsibly.

    100% reliable, traceable and insured. All packages are insured by UPS. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your request a quote, and your payment is issued within 2 days of having your laptop delivered to us.
