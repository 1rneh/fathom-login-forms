ModCloth is pleased to support the Billing and Shipping of your order to an Armed Forces APO or FPO address. To take advantage of this service, please enter either APO or FPO in the 'City' field, United States in the 'Country' field, and the APO/FPO region in the 'State' field. All APO and FPO orders will be shipped via the U.S. Postal Service. “Express” and “Next Day” service is not available.
