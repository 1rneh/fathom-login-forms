How to choose a strong password
  • Passwords must be at least 8 characters
  • Passwords must consist of at least two of the following:
    - capital letter
    - lowercase letter
    - number
    - limited special characters, such as !@#$%^&+=
  • Avoid passwords that are easy to guess, for example, "Password"
  • Remember, passwords are case sensitive. For instance, "Rivers2!%" and "rivers2!%" are two different passwords.
