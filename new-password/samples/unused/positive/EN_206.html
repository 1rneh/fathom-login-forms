Frequently asked questions

Why subscribe to become a Member?

Because you'll get extra extra every day.

As a Digital or Digital + Delivery Member you'll get unrestricted access to journalists you know and trust across web and app on your favourite devices PLUS there are heaps of unreal +Rewards, like special offers, extras and exclusives, as well as a tonne of discounts and unique experiences to enjoy. They're worth hundreds of dollars a year and are yours as a Digital or Digital + Delivery Member.

As a App Only Member you'll get unrestricted access on The app, with thousands of stories, live sport scores, games and much more, it's the best way to never miss what matters to you and it's right at your fingertips.

Who gets to enjoy Member's benefits and +Rewards?

Do you have a Digital membership with your papers home delivered or just a standalone Digital membership to The Daily Telegraph, Herald Sun, The Courier-Mail or The Advertiser? If so, then you're already an eligible Member and can enjoy all the exclusive benefits and +Rewards that comes with your Membership.

This however, doesn't include App Only Members, print only Subscribers, or people with a subscription to PerthNow, Supercoach, iTunes, Google Play Newsstand or a foxsports.com.au. And for those with a Teachers or Uni Students subscription, or on a Connected Reader subscription, sorry you're not eligible either. But if you want to upgrade your membership to one which includes +Rewards, please call us on 1300 My News (1300696397).

How long does it take for my home delivery to start? (Only applies if home delivery is included in your package)

It could take up to 5 business days before your first paper delivery arrives. But once your delivery is activated, you'll get the next edition, hot off the press.

Can I put my subscription or membership on hold?

The beauty of having a digital subscription or membership is that it travels with you wherever you go. But if your subscription or membership includes home delivery, then you can request to suspend your paper delivery through My Account.

Why should I pay for a digital membership when I can access news for free on other sites?

The answer is simple. Only Digital Members enjoy unrestricted access to stories, insight and analysis from our expert journalists, across your favourite devices.

What's more Digital and Digital + Delivery Members get exclusive extras, benefits and +Rewards. And all this great value far outweighs the small subscription price

What if I'm out of the home delivery area? (Only applies if home delivery is included in your package)

If your address is in a non-serviceable area, we'll contact you to discuss your options.

What sites can you access as part of our News+ Network?

Our News+ Network is made up of our most popular news sites, like dailytelegraph.com.au, heraldsun.com.au, couriermail.com.au and advertiser.com.au. As a Digital Member, you get access to them all PLUS townsvillebulletin.com.au, cairnspost.com.au, goldcoastbulletin.com.au, geelongadvertiser.com.au, ntnews.com.au, themercury.com.au. Simply use your subscription account to log in.

This however, doesn't include App Only Members, print only Subscribers, or people with a subscription to PerthNow, SuperCoach, iTunes, Google Play Newsstand or a foxsports.com.au. But if you want to upgrade your membership to one which includes our News+ Network, please call us on 1300 My News (1300696397).
