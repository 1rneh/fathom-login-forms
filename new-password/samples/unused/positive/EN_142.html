    Making up a secure password

    We recommend that you choose a sentence, then use the first letter of each word and the punctuation to create a passphrase that has upper and lower case letters and special characters.

    The phraseThe password
    My dog's birthday is April 7. When is yours?Md'sbiA7.Wiy?
    I've been to Hawaii 3 times in my life.IbtH3ximl.

    Your password can’t use your user ID and shouldn’t contain:

    • dictionary words in any language
    • your or your family’s personal information (birthdates, Social Security number, past or current phone numbers, Health/Medical Record number)
    • 4 or more characters in a sequence, or repeated (1234, abcd, aaaa, or 2222)
    Passwords must be at least 8 characters long.
