about us

We are a value-added battery and power product distributor focusing on advanced battery technologies, LED lighting and custom power solutions. Our inventory includes more than 3000 different products offered by over 80 manufacturers. Strategic locations and distribution ensures that in stock items are received quickly. With direct manufacturer relationships we also offer design and manufacturing solutions. Our company sells to the public, retailers, distributors, OEMS, government facilities worldwide and WE WANT YOU AS A CUSTOMER!
