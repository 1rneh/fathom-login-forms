Replacement remote controls

Original replacement remote controls are identical to those ones supplied with your new audio/visual device. When you buy a new television from a reputable dealer, the remote control which comes with it is known as an original remote.



More Info

Alternative Replacements

These remote controls are model specific and will work as soon as you insert the batteries. No set up is required - not to be confused with universal remote controls which usually need to be set up and have limited functionality. We supply the Classic brand of replacement remote control which is of a very high quality. The Classic brand is well established and is used by consumers and service industries worldwide.

Blu-Ray remotes

Blu-Ray players have surged in popularity in recent years, with super crisp HD movies now affordable for pretty much everyone. However, replacement Blu-Ray remote controls need to be just as affordable as well, which is why we stock a wide range from the major brands at very affordable prices. Plus, they include full functionality and work out of the box so you can enjoy the full high definition TV experience once again from the comfort of your own sofa.

DVD player remote controls

DVD players are almost as common as Televisions these days, so we make sure we're thoroughly stocked in the DVD remote department as well! In 1993, it was only the likes of Phillips and Sony who were leading the development of DVD players, but with their vast popularity, we now stock every brand including many others like LG remote controls and Technika remote controls.

Home theatre remotes

Our range of home theatre remote controls cover older devices right up to the latest systems, so you can get a replacement for the perfect sound experience on a whim with just a few clicks. This includes 2 channel, 5.1 channel and all the way up to 9 channel systems from the world's biggest brands like Sony, LG and more. Plus, with device-specific alternative and original controllers, they'll work with full functionality and no programming required.

Set top box remotes

Another highly popular audiovisual device is the set top box, or STB. Whilst originally reserved for the likes of Virgin Media or Sky, generic devices have have become commonplace with the likes of Freeview and Freesat gaining ground for free digital TV. One of our most popular brands in stock for these include Bush remote controls from the well-known audiovisual manufacturer. We also stock Humax remote controls as a popular choice for set top box remotes across the UK. Our range includes the older models right up to the latest for Foxsat and the HDR digital recorder devices.

Projector and camcorder remotes

Although camcorder and projector remotes may not appear to be as popular as some other types, they are extremely useful as anyone with either of these devices will be aware. After all, if your project is on the ceiling, or you need to use a camcorder from a distance, they are simply essential. However, there is one problem with these: they're usually very small! This makes them easier to misplace or drop, which is why we ensure that our customers can buy replacement controls for a huge range of projector and camcorder devices.

VCR remotes

VCR's do not dominate the market as they did in the 1990s, and because of that, it's even harder to find an original manufacturer or alternative manufacturer VCR remote control replacement. However, video players are still used by some these days, so we make sure that we're well-stocked with VCR player remotes from all of the major brands, including Hitachi remote controls and Logik remote controls as well.

Surround sound, hi-fi and sound bar remotes

Surround sound, hi-fi and sound bar remote controls tend to be quite small, making them easier to misplace or break. However, we stock a wide range from all the top brands so you can quickly receive a like-for-like handset which allows you to enjoy your favourite music or TV sound system once again.
